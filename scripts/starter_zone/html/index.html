WELCOME TO NO LOVE LOST
Welcome to No Love Lost. Before leaving the city, every new citizen must complete the New Citizen Checklist. This process confirms your identity, issues your starter essentials, connects you with the Job Center, and makes sure you are ready for serious roleplay before leaving the starter boundary. Until every checklist requirement is complete, you will remain within city limits and will not be cleared to leave.
📋
CHECKLIST CLEARANCE
01Complete each checklist task before attempting to leave the city.
02Register your ID and license so your character is properly recorded in city systems.
03Select your starter essentials carefully. These items are intended to support your first steps in the city.
04Stay active, build your bank balance, and finish the required bike distance before city clearance is granted.
💼
JOB CENTER
01Go to the Job Center and speak with the counselor to choose your first city job.
02Your first job choice completes the Job Center checklist task.
03You can return to the Job Center later to switch between available city jobs.
04Rural and long-route jobs unlock after you are cleared to leave the city.
